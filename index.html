What is Hackathon 2025?

Hackathon 2025 is a state-level innovation challenge aimed at bringing together developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and students to solve pressing real-world problems using technology.

Participants will compete in teams, guided by experienced mentors and evaluated by a distinguished jury of industry leaders, policymakers, and domain experts.

Telangana: India's Emerging Tech Powerhouse

Home to HITEC City, T-Hub, and a vibrant startup ecosystem, Telangana is at the forefront of tech innovation through initiatives like TSIC, TASK, and WE Hub. The state is actively fostering digital transformation and entrepreneurship.
