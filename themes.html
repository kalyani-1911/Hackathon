About the Hackathon

Join the state's brightest minds to build solutions that shape tomorrow. Telangana Hackathon 2025 focuses on key thematic areas aligned with the state's development goals.

Empower Innovation. Transform the Future.

The Telangana Hackathon 2025 brings together students, developers, and innovators from across the state to solve real-world challenges through technology-driven solutions.

Our themes were carefully selected through consultations with domain experts, government officials, and community stakeholders to address the most pressing needs of our society.

Event Timeline

Important dates and milestones for Telangana Hackathon 2025

June 1, 2025

Registrations Open

Online registration begins for all participants. Teams can register through our portal.

July 15, 2025

Registration Deadline

Last date to register for the hackathon. All teams must submit their basic details.

July 30, 2025

Theme Selection

Teams must finalize and submit their chosen theme and problem statement.

August 15-17, 2025

Hackathon Event

36-hour coding marathon at T-Hub, Hyderabad. All teams work on their solutions.

August 17, 2025

Judging & Awards

Final presentations and judging. Winners announced and prizes distributed.

Healthcare Innovation

×

Theme Description

This theme focuses on leveraging technology to revolutionize healthcare access and delivery, particularly for underserved communities in Telangana.

Problem Statements

  • AI-powered early disease detection systems for rural areas
  • Telemedicine platforms connecting rural patients with specialists
  • Mental health support and counseling applications
  • IoT-based remote patient monitoring solutions
  • Blockchain for secure medical records sharing
  • AR/VR applications for medical training
  • Automated diagnostic tools for common diseases

Expected Outcomes

Solutions should aim to improve healthcare accessibility, reduce costs, and enhance the quality of care, particularly in rural and semi-urban areas of Telangana.

Technology Focus Areas

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Mobile Applications
  • Data Analytics
  • Blockchain
