Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the timeline

Can I register after the deadline? +

We strongly encourage registering before the May 20 deadline. Late registrations may be accepted on a case-by-case basis depending on availability.

What if my team can't attend all 36 hours? +

While we encourage full participation, we understand teams may need breaks. At least one team member should be present at all times during the event.

When will we receive mentorship? +

Mentors will be available throughout the hackathon, with scheduled sessions on Day 2. You can also request mentor assistance anytime via our help desk.

What are the hardware requirements? +

Participants should bring their own laptops and chargers. We'll provide internet access, power strips, and basic hardware components for IoT projects.
